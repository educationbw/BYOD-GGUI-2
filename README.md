## BYOD Setup
To use your own custom domain after deploying to one of the platforms above:

### 1. Using Netlify
* After clicking the button above and connecting your GitHub, go to your **Site Settings**.
* Select **Domain Management** > **Add custom domain**.
* Enter your domain (e.g., `myproxy.com`) and follow the DNS instructions.

### 2. Using Vercel
* Once the clone is complete, go to your **Project Dashboard**.
* Click **Settings** > **Domains**.
* Add your domain and follow the verification steps.
